The World Data Bank II outline files are digitized from navigational charts to a working scale of approximately 1:3 Million, and represent the "state of the art" in the mid 1970s. There are three files composing the WDB-II set on this CD-ROM: coastlines, rivers and lakes, and political boundaries (the last becoming increasingly obsolete!). Some of the details contained in the original WDB files, such as "order" of the elements (as related to size and importance of islands or lakes) have been left out of these versions -- the full and official WDB is available from the National Technical Information Service, Springfield, VA.
